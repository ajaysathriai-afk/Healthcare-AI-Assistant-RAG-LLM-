Title: Acidity / GERD (Acid Reflux)

What it is
GERD occurs when stomach acid flows back into the food pipe (esophagus), causing burning and discomfort.

Symptoms
- Burning in chest (heartburn)
- Sour taste in mouth
- Burping, bloating
- Throat irritation
- Chronic cough (sometimes)

Triggers
- Spicy foods
- Fried foods
- Coffee, alcohol
- Eating late at night
- Obesity
- Stress

Lifestyle management
- Eat smaller meals
- Avoid lying down 2–3 hours after eating
- Raise head of bed
- Reduce spicy/fatty foods
- Weight management

Red flags
- Difficulty swallowing
- Unexplained weight loss
- Vomiting blood or black stools
- Severe chest pain (rule out heart problem)

Important
Chest pain should not be ignored — heart-related pain can be dangerous.
