Title: Mental Health Basics — Stress, Anxiety, Depression

Stress symptoms
- Irritability
- Sleep problems
- Headaches
- Poor concentration

Anxiety symptoms
- Excess worry
- Palpitations
- Sweating
- Restlessness

Depression symptoms
- Low mood
- Loss of interest
- Hopelessness
- Sleep/appetite changes

Emergency help needed
If a person has thoughts of self-harm or suicide, seek professional help immediately.

Note
Mental health is treatable. Talking to a counselor/doctor is recommended.
