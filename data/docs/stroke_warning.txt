Title: Stroke Warning Signs (Emergency)

Stroke occurs when blood flow to brain is blocked or bleeding occurs.

FAST warning signs
F - Face drooping
A - Arm weakness
S - Speech difficulty
T - Time to call emergency

Other symptoms
- Sudden confusion
- Sudden loss of vision
- Sudden severe headache
- Loss of balance

Action
Stroke is an emergency. Immediate treatment can save life and prevent disability.
