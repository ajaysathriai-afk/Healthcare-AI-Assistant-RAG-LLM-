FEVER (General information)

Definition:
- Fever means body temperature above ~38°C.

Common causes:
- Viral infections (cold, flu)
- Bacterial infections
- Dehydration/heat exhaustion
- Inflammation

Common symptoms:
- High temperature
- Chills
- Sweating
- Body pains
- Weakness

Home care:
- Drink enough fluids
- Rest
- Lukewarm sponge bath if needed
- Paracetamol can be used for fever (avoid overdose)

See doctor urgently if:
- Fever > 3 days
- Very high fever (>40°C)
- Breathing difficulty
- Severe headache/stiff neck
- Confusion or seizures
- Severe dehydration
