Title: Fever in Children — Extra Caution

Fever in children is common but dehydration risk is high.

Home care
- Fluids (ORS)
- Light food
- Monitor urine output

Red flags
- Child <3 months with fever
- Seizures
- Difficulty breathing
- Not waking up properly
- Persistent vomiting
- Signs of dehydration

Note
In young children, fever requires careful monitoring.
