Title: Diarrhea — Home Care and Danger Signs

Overview
Diarrhea is frequent loose stools. Commonly due to infections, food intolerance, or medications.

Main risks
Dehydration is the biggest danger, especially in children and elderly.

Home care
- ORS (oral rehydration solution)
- Clean water and light foods
- Avoid oily/spicy foods
- Continue feeding children

Signs of dehydration
- Dry mouth, extreme thirst
- Less urine, dark urine
- Dizziness
- Sunken eyes
- Lethargy

Red flags
- Blood in stools
- High fever
- Severe abdominal pain
- Diarrhea > 2 days in adults
- Any diarrhea in infants with dehydration symptoms

Note
Antibiotics are not needed for most viral diarrhea.
