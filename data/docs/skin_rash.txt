Title: Skin Rash — Common Causes

Causes
- Allergy
- Infection
- Heat rash
- Insect bites
- Eczema

Care
- Avoid scratching
- Gentle moisturizer
- Avoid suspected triggers

Red flags
- Rash with fever
- Severe swelling of face/lips
- Difficulty breathing
- Rapid spreading rash
- Blisters/skin peeling

Note
Severe allergy can be life-threatening.
