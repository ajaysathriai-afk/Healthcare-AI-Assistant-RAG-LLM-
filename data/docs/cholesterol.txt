Title: Cholesterol — Basics

What is cholesterol?
Cholesterol is a fat-like substance in blood. High LDL increases risk of heart disease.

Types
- LDL: “bad”
- HDL: “good”
- Triglycerides: another blood fat

Lifestyle control
- Reduce fried/processed foods
- Increase fiber (oats, fruits, vegetables)
- Exercise
- Weight management

Note
Only blood test confirms cholesterol. Medicines may be needed for high risk individuals.
