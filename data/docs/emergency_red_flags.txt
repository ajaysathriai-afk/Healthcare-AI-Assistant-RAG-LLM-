Title: Medical Emergency Red Flags

Seek emergency medical help immediately if:
- Severe chest pain
- Trouble breathing
- Sudden weakness/numbness one side
- Severe headache with confusion
- Uncontrolled bleeding
- Seizures
- Loss of consciousness
- Severe allergic reaction (swelling lips/face)
- Suicidal thoughts or self-harm risk

Note
This list is not complete. If symptoms feel severe or unusual, seek emergency care.
