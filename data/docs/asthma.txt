Title: Asthma — Symptoms and Attack Warning Signs

Asthma basics
Asthma causes narrowing and inflammation of airways leading to breathing difficulty.

Symptoms
- Wheezing
- Shortness of breath
- Chest tightness
- Night cough

Triggers
- Dust, pollen
- Smoke
- Cold air
- Exercise
- Respiratory infections

Red flags (urgent)
- Severe breathing difficulty
- Unable to speak full sentences
- Bluish lips
- No relief from inhaler
- Drowsiness/confusion

Note
Asthma requires proper diagnosis and treatment plan. Inhalers must be used correctly.
