Title: Pregnancy Safety — General Information

General advice
Pregnancy needs careful medical supervision. Avoid self-medication.

Common safe steps
- Prenatal checkups
- Iron/folate as prescribed
- Nutritious diet
- Hydration and rest

Danger signs (seek urgent care)
- Vaginal bleeding
- Severe abdominal pain
- Severe headache/blurred vision
- Swelling hands/face
- Reduced fetal movement
- High fever

Note
Only a doctor can advise medicines safely in pregnancy.
