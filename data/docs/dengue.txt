Title: Dengue Fever — Symptoms and Red Flags

Dengue is a mosquito-borne viral illness.

Symptoms
- High fever
- Severe body/joint pain (“breakbone fever”)
- Headache, eye pain
- Rash
- Fatigue

Danger: Dengue hemorrhagic fever
Red flags
- Bleeding gums/nose
- Black stools
- Severe abdominal pain
- Persistent vomiting
- Extreme weakness

Care
- Hydration is critical
- Paracetamol may be used
- Avoid aspirin/ibuprofen unless doctor says so

Note
Seek medical care for warning signs.
