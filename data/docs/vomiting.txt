Title: Vomiting — Causes and Care

Common causes
- Food poisoning
- Viral gastroenteritis
- Motion sickness
- Pregnancy
- Migraine
- Acid reflux

Home care
- Small sips of water/ORS
- Avoid heavy food for some hours
- Eat light (banana, rice, toast)
- Rest

Red flags
- Vomiting blood
- Severe headache + vomiting
- Severe abdominal pain
- Signs of dehydration
- Persistent vomiting > 24 hours
- Vomiting in infants

Note
If vomiting is severe, dehydration can become dangerous quickly.
