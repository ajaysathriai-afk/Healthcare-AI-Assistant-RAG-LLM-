Title: First Aid — Basics

General first aid
- Keep calm
- Check breathing
- Stop bleeding with pressure
- Clean wounds with clean water

Burns
- Cool with running water (10–20 min)
- Do not apply toothpaste/oil
- Cover with clean cloth

Fainting
- Lay person down
- Raise legs
- Check breathing

Emergency
Call emergency services for severe bleeding, unconsciousness, breathing difficulty.
