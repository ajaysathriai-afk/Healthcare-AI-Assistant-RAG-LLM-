Title: Cough — Causes and Care

What is cough?
Cough is a protective reflex to clear mucus, irritants, or infections from the throat and lungs.

Types
- Dry cough: irritation or viral infection
- Wet/productive cough: mucus present
- Chronic cough: > 8 weeks

Common causes
- Common cold / viral infection
- Allergies
- Asthma
- Acid reflux (GERD)
- Smoking / pollution
- Bronchitis
- Pneumonia (serious)

Basic home care
- Warm fluids, honey (not for children <1 year)
- Steam inhalation
- Gargling warm salt water
- Avoid smoke/dust
- Rest and hydration

When to see a doctor (Red flags)
- Cough > 2 weeks
- Blood in cough
- Severe chest pain
- Difficulty breathing / wheezing
- High fever
- Weight loss / night sweats
- Blue lips or severe weakness

Note
Cough medicines have limited effectiveness for viral cough. Persistent cough needs evaluation.
