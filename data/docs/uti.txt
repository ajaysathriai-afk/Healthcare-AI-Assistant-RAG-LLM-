Title: UTI (Urinary Tract Infection)

Symptoms
- Burning while urinating
- Frequent urination
- Lower abdominal pain
- Cloudy urine
- Fever (suggests spreading infection)

Home care
- Hydration
- Avoid holding urine

Red flags
- Fever with back pain
- Blood in urine
- UTI during pregnancy

Note
UTIs often require antibiotics.
