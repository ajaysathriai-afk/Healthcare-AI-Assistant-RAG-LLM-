Title: Headache — Common Types and When to Worry

Common types
- Tension headache: stress, dehydration, poor sleep
- Migraine: throbbing pain, nausea, sensitivity to light
- Sinus headache: facial pressure, nasal congestion

Home management
- Hydration
- Rest and sleep
- Reduce screen time
- Cold/warm compress
- Avoid skipping meals

Triggers
- Stress
- Lack of sleep
- Dehydration
- Excess caffeine or caffeine withdrawal
- Long screen exposure

Red flags (seek emergency care)
- Sudden “worst headache of life”
- Headache with weakness, confusion, slurred speech
- Headache with high fever + stiff neck
- After head injury
- With vision loss
- Severe vomiting

Note
Frequent headaches need proper diagnosis.
