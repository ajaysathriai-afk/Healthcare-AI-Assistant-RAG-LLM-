Title: Typhoid Fever — Overview

Typhoid is a bacterial infection often spread through contaminated food/water.

Symptoms
- Prolonged fever
- Weakness
- Abdominal pain
- Constipation or diarrhea
- Loss of appetite

Care
Typhoid usually needs antibiotics prescribed by a doctor.

Red flags
- Confusion
- Severe abdominal swelling/pain
- Bleeding
- Persistent vomiting

Prevention
- Clean drinking water
- Hygiene
- Vaccination (where advised)
