Title: Chest Pain — Do Not Ignore

Chest pain causes range from mild to life-threatening.

Possible causes
- Acid reflux
- Muscle strain
- Anxiety/panic attack
- Heart attack (critical)
- Lung infection, pulmonary embolism

Heart attack warning signs
- Heavy pressure-like chest pain
- Pain radiating to arm/jaw/back
- Sweating, nausea
- Shortness of breath
- Feeling of doom

Action
If chest pain is severe or suspicious of heart causes, seek emergency help immediately.

Note
Do not delay evaluation for chest pain.
