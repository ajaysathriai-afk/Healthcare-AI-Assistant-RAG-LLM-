Title: Sore Throat — Causes and Warning Signs

Common causes
- Viral infection (most common)
- Bacterial infection (strep throat)
- Acid reflux
- Allergies, dry air
- Smoking

Symptoms
- Pain while swallowing
- Dry scratchy throat
- Fever
- Swollen glands in neck
- White patches on tonsils (can suggest bacterial infection)

Home care
- Warm salt water gargles
- Warm fluids
- Adequate rest
- Humidifier

Red flags
- Difficulty breathing
- Drooling (inability to swallow)
- Severe one-sided pain
- Fever > 3 days
- Rash
- Swelling in neck

Note
Bacterial throat infections may need antibiotics. Avoid self-medicating antibiotics.
